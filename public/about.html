About this page

This site publishes a concise, UK-focused daily brief on AI. It aggregates public RSS feeds (e.g., DSIT, NCSC, ICO, CMA, Ofcom, UKRI, BBC, The Register, Reuters) and asks an AI model to draft a short summary strictly from the feed titles/snippets.

  • Update cadence: daily (typically morning UK time).
  • Scope: UK policy, regulators, public sector guidance, research funding, UK companies; plus global AI news with clear UK implications.
  • Limitations: We avoid speculation and keep to information present in sources. If items conflict, we note uncertainty.
  • Attribution: Source links appear on the page; click to read originals.
  • Privacy: No tracking; static HTML.
