About this site

This site publishes concise daily AI briefs from public RSS/Atom feeds and asks an AI model to draft short summaries strictly from feed titles/snippets.

  • Update cadence: daily (typically morning UK time).
  • Tabs: UK AI, AI & Business, AI & Work, Global AI, AI Security, Ethics & Policy.
  • Attribution: Source links appear on each tab; click to read originals.
  • Limitations: Summaries only reflect what appears in the feed titles/snippets. If coverage is thin, we may broaden sources and note it.
  • Privacy: No tracking; static HTML.
